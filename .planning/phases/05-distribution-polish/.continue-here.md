| Defense-in-depth filters that diverge | Worker classifier and UI mirror were both noise-filtering on authorType; Fix 3 alone left UI dropping messages. | blocking | When two filter layers gate identical data, share the predicate (export shared TS function) or comment-link them in both files. |
